    Curriculum

    Experiential Learning

    Attaining the core literacy and numeracy skills is essential, but too is the application of these skills in experiential based learning scenarios where children learn by doing. Our experiential learning approach draws elements from the philosophy of early learning that recognizes the importance of the voice of the child and the importance of learning through experiencing, exploring and interacting. Our classroom and experienced faculty provide opportunities to learn by doing, take risks and apply new skills to ensure that every child is stimulated and challenged to achieve its best.

     

    Stimulating Environment

    Our physical surroundings have a profound impact on our capacity to learn, our architecturally designed classrooms are light filled and technologically rich which offer exciting learning opportunities of every child. Xavier’s Child Development Centre offers an inspiring academic culture, a happy environment that values creative and diverse thinking and plenty of room to run, play, explore, invent and imagine.

     

    Strong Foundation

    Fundamental to learning in the Child Development Centre is the establishment of highly proficient literacy and numeracy skills to support the development of critical thinking, deep learning and higher order conceptual understandings. Through our differentiated teaching, we understand your child’s areas of strength and weakness and then provide opportunities for each child to be challenged and stimulated in its journey of learning.
