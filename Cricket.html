    Cricket
     

    Cricket is a game which challenges the physical and mental skills whilst developing character, self- esteem, teamwork, sportsmanship and tolerance.
    The school provides students the opportunity to learn and develop their sports skills in a safe and friendly environment.

    FEATURES:
    • Skill-intensive sessions in batting, bowling and fielding
    • Specialised coaching for pace and spin bowlers and wicket keepers.
    • Accredited and friendly coaches
    • Individually reports on all players
    • Exciting cricket activities, competitions
